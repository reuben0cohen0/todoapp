Get Motivaited!

  1. Set an achiveable task for the time period you have
  2. Have a positive mindset e.g. "I can smash this out of the park or' 'I can do all this today'
  3. Minimise distractions - put the phone on silent or away from you where you cant get distracted
  4. Think about the good stuff that will happen after you get it done quick not coming bck later to finsih it off
  5. Feed your brain with good food and water
  6. Put some thought to the outcome - How will it affect others? What is the benefit of getting it done?
  7. If it's looking too big, break it up into bitesized chunks.
